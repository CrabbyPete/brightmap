Currently, BrightMap works exclusively with Eventbrite. In order for us to find out which of your community members are interested in purchasing which services, we add a survey question to your Eventbrite registration form.

In order to generate and sell leads on BrightMap, you must grant permission to Brightmap to access your Eventbrite page. This is so that we can add the survey question to your registration form and read the results from the Eventbrite database. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Log into your Eventbrite page
  2. Click on the Account tab
  3. Click on Multi-User Access
  4. Click "Add Email Address"
  5. Enter the following settings
    • Email: <community name>@brightmap.com (eg "my.community@brightmap.com")
    • Grant access to: All events
    • Actions to perform: Selected actions only
    • Edit event details not including the payment options
    • Edit survey questions
    • View order and attendee reports (read-only)
    • Receive copies of: No emails
  6. Click Save

Once you click Save, Eventbrite will notify us that your account has been setup for BrightMap. We will add survey questions and send you reports of the leads you're generating, sponsors who want to buy your leads, and the cash balance accumulating in your account as these leads are sold.

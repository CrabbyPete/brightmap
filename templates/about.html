About BrightMap

 

 

BrightMap is a community-driven lead-generation system.

 

Community organizers use BrightMap to monetize their events and provide a valuable introduction service for their members. Service providers use BrightMap to get introduced to people interested in buying their services.

 

BrightMap was founded in 2011 by Graham Lawlor and Peter Douma.

 

For more information, contact info@brightmap.com

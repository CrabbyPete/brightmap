Best Practices for Event Organizers

 

Lead Categories/Interests

  • Think of what your community buys or influences the purchase of
  • Think of the service providers that show up at your event already
  • Think of the people you already send referrals to
  • Think of the service provider types your community asks for referrals to

The best lead categories are for services that

  • People research the purchase online (or through community leaders such as businesses networking event organizers, where credibility/references are important)
  • But when it comes time to buy, they usually go through a live person (rather than purchasing online)


So good lead-gen categories are things like: cars, houses, insurance, banks, schools, lawyers, accountants, web development agencies, hosting providers, etc

Bad lead-gen categories are things like: books/newspapers/magazines, bricks & mortar retail establishments such as bars/restaurants, anything you can find on Amazon, etc

Promoting your Lead-Buyers

  • Announce / feature them at the event (mention them from the stage/podium, sponsor logo in program slides/handouts, etc)
  • Write back to any responses from attendees who copy you

Integration with Meetup.com

  • Create meeting at meetup. Close off RSVP
  • Send an email to the list and direct people to Eventbrite directly

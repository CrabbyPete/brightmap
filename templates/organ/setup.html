Join BrightMap - Eventbrite Setup

In order to generate and sell leads on BrightMap, you must permission the system to access your Eventbrite page.
This is so that we can add the survey question to your registration form and read the results from the Eventbrite database.
To do this, follow these steps:

  • STEP 1 of 3
  • STEP 2 of 3
  • STEP 3 of 3

1Add BrightMap to your Eventbrite Page (click here)

If this link does not work, log in to your Eventbrite.com page, click on the Account tab, Click on Multi-User Access, and then click the "Add Email Address" button

2Enter the following settings

  • Email: <brightmap login>@brightmap.com
    (eg "my.community@brightmap.com")
  • Grant access to: All events
  • Actions to perform:Selected actions only
    • Edit event details not including the payment options
    • Edit survey questions
    • View order and attendee reports (read-only)
  • Receive copies of: No emails

3Click Save

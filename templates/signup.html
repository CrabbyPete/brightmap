Currently, BrightMap can only be used by event organizers using Eventbrite. In order for BrightMap to make introductions between your attendees, you must grant it limited access to your Eventbrite page. The only permissions BrightMap needs, are a) to add the survey question to your event registration form (so your attendees can request introductions) and b) to read the results of this survey form (so we can make the introductions). Adding BrightMap to your Eventbrite page takes about 1 minute, by following these steps:

  1. Go to your Eventbrite Multi-User settings (click here or follow these steps)
    • Open your Eventbrite dashboard
    • Click on the Account tab (top nav)
    • Click on Multi-User Access (left nav)
    • Click "Add Email Address" (green button)
  2. Enter the following settings
    • Email: <community name>@brightmap.com (eg "my.community@brightmap.com")
    • Grant access to: All events
    • Actions to perform: Selected actions only
    • Click Deselect All Actions
    • Check Edit survey questions
    • Check Manage orders and attendees
    • Receive copies of: No emails
  3. Click Save

Once you click Save, Eventbrite will notify us that your account has been setup for BrightMap. We will contact you to determine how the survey question will read on your Eventbrite page, and who the introductions will go to.

If you have any questions, feel free to email support@brightmap.com at any time.
